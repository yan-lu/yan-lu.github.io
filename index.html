About me
  • I am an Applied Robotics Scientist at Amazon Lab126 developing cutting-edge technologies for ground-breaking projects. Before joining Amazon, I had a great time at NVIDIA working on visual localization and mapping for autonomous driving. I have eight years' research experience on robot navigation with a focus on SLAM (simultaneous localization and mapping). I obtained my Ph.D. from Texas A&M University in 2015, my MPhil from Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2010, and my B.S. from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2008.
