About me
  • I am a research scientist at Honda Research Institute USA working on localization and mapping for autonomous driving. I have over seven years' research experience on robot navigation with a focus on SLAM (simultaneous localization and mapping). I obtained my Ph.D. from Texas A&M University in 2015, my MPhil from Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2010, and my B.S. from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2008.
